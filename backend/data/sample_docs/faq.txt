Frequently Asked Questions - Customer Support

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email address and we will send you a password reset link within 5 minutes. The link expires after 24 hours.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: You can cancel your subscription at any time from your Account Settings page. Navigate to Settings > Subscription > Cancel Plan. Your access will continue until the end of the current billing period. No refunds are issued for partial months.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely via Stripe. We do not store your card details on our servers.

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days. Overnight shipping is available for an additional fee. International orders may take 10-15 business days depending on customs.

Q: Can I get a refund?
A: Yes. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. To request a refund, contact support@example.com with your order number. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days back to your original payment method.

Q: How do I contact customer support?
A: You can reach our support team via email at support@example.com, by phone at 1-800-555-0100 (Mon-Fri, 9am-6pm EST), or through the live chat on our website.

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes. We use AES-256 encryption for all stored data and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We are GDPR and CCPA compliant. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

Q: How do I update my billing information?
A: Go to Account Settings > Billing > Update Payment Method. You can add a new card or update your existing billing details at any time.

Q: What happens if my payment fails?
A: If a payment fails, we will retry it after 3 days and again after 7 days. You will receive an email notification each time. If payment is not resolved within 14 days, your account will be temporarily suspended.

Q: Do you offer a free trial?
A: Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card is required to start the trial. You will be prompted to add a payment method before the trial ends if you wish to continue.
